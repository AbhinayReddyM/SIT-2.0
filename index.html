GITAM QUIZ CLUB Presents

SOUTH INDIAN THALI 2.0


Experience the vibrant culture of South Indian cinema with South Indian Thali 2.0.
An exciting event that celebrates the magic of South Indian movies!
This unique gathering combines entertainment, culture,
and fun-filled activities,
inspired by the most iconic elements of South Indian films.

Countdown:

DIALOGUE DEBATE

Participants will form teams of 2-3 members. Each team will engage in a dialogue exchange with an opposing team. The objective is to maintain the dialogue without hesitation, repetition, or running out of ideas. The team that falters first loses the round.

Register

GUESS THE CHARACTER

Step into the world of cinema and put your knowledge to the test in Guess the Character! In this exciting and fast-paced event, participants will be shown a image of the character and your mission is simple: guess the name of the character!

Register

REVERSE RHYTHM

Step into the rhythm of fun and challenge! Participate in our Reverse Rhythm Activity, test your timing and memory with unique patterns, and master the beats to become a rhythm champion! Are you ready to groove?

Register

TRAILER CUT

Unleash your creativity and editing skills in the Trailer Cut Challenge! Participants must create a captivating movie trailer that captures the essence of the entire film(from only one movie). The trailer should provide an engaging glimpse into the storyline, characters, and key moments, leaving viewers excited and curious about the movie.

Register

SCRIPT WRITING

Dive into the world of South Indian cinema with the Sequel Story Guess Challenge! In this activity, participants will use their imagination and knowledge of famous South Indian movies to guess or predict the sequel storyline and the short narration of the story.

Register

ROLEPLAY

Participants will immerse themselves in a roleplay activity where they re-create and act out a specific scene from a movie. The goal is to bring the characters to life, understand their emotions, and interpret the scene creatively while staying true to the context.

Register

MEME MASTERS

Unleash your creativity in our meme-making competition! Design unique and entertaining memes to stand out and win exciting prizes. Let the laughter begin!

Register

QUIZ COMPETITION

Gear up for an exciting battle of brains! Join our Movie Quiz Competition and showcase your expertise on iconic films, legendary actors, and unforgettable moments from the silver screen while having loads of fun."

About

GITAM Quiz Club – where knowledge meets entertainment! 🎓✨ Known for its dynamic and engaging quiz events, the club has become the ultimate hub for trivia enthusiasts and curious minds on campus. From thought-provoking quizzes to fun-filled entertainment rounds, the GITAM Quiz Club has successfully hosted numerous blockbuster events, leaving participants thrilled and craving more.
